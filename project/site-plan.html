Apex Dental Care — Site Plan

WDD 231 | Individual Project Plan

Site Name

Name: Apex Dental Care

Reason: "Apex" represents top-tier quality while subtly referencing the root tip of a tooth-a professional yet clever nod to dentistry.

Site Purpose

To create a clean, modern digital storefront for a local dentist office. It will help patients view services, find contact details, and seamlessly request appointments using an interactive popup modal form. To be clean, minimal, modern, and professional.

Target Audience Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: "I'm a new patient in the area. How can I easily request an appointment online?"
  • Scenario 2: "I have a toothache and it's after hours. Where can I quickly find the office phone number and location?"

Color Scheme

This palette uses clean, calming medical tones to build patient trust and reduce anxiety:

Primary: Navy Blue (#0D3B66) - Headers & Titles
Secondary: Teal (#00B4D8) - Accents & Buttons
Background: Off-White (#F8F9FA) - Main Canvas

Typography

  • Headings: Montserrat (Bold, modern, and clinical)
  • Body Text: Open Sans (Clean, friendly, and highly readable on screens)
